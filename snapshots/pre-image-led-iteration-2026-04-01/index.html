How It Works

A clear process, handled with close attention.

Initial consultation

We begin with a conversation about your child, current school context, the areas of concern, and what a good outcome would actually look like.

02

Tutor matching

We recommend a tutor whose subject depth and teaching manner fit the student, rather than defaulting to the fastest available option.

03

Tuition begins with a plan

Sessions start with clear priorities, and we remain available to review what is working well and what should change as the term progresses.
